Core Event

Quarterly Stock Pitch Competition

The Quarterly Stock Pitch Competition (SPC) is a cornerstone event that provides members with an opportunity to showcase their growth and learning throughout their time at SIG. Held quarterly, teams analyze companies and present stock pitches to industry professionals. Judges evaluate pitches across Equity Research, Quantitative Analysis, and Sustainability dimensions, providing valuable feedback to help members refine their analytical and presentation skills.

Quarterly Team Competition
Core Event

Quantathon

A quarterly event where Quantitative Analysis committee members present their work and receive feedback from industry specialists. The Quantathon showcases advanced statistical models, programming projects, and quantitative research that drives SIG's investment decisions. Analysts present their findings on portfolio construction, risk management, and ESG integration, receiving expert guidance to further refine their quantitative methods.

Core Event

Sustainability Showcase

A quarterly research exhibition by the Sustainability Committee, focusing on ESG scoring methodologies and sustainability characteristics of industries. The showcase highlights interdisciplinary research combining environmental, social, and governance factors with financial analysis. Industry professionals are invited to provide advice and feedback, helping members develop cutting-edge ESG evaluation frameworks that inform sustainable investment decisions.

Quarterly Research Exhibition
External Event

Data Fest

SIG was the first UCSD organization to send members to the annual UCLA Datafest in Los Angeles. This prestigious event brings together teams from universities across the region to compete in data analysis challenges. SIG members participate in this intensive competition, applying their quantitative skills to real-world datasets and collaborating with peers from other institutions, further strengthening our commitment to data-driven investment analysis.

Beach Clean-Up Social & Shake Shack Fundy

To support the value of sustainability, SIG collaborated with the Associated Students Office of Local Affairs and the Surf Club to organize and host a beach clean-up, supporting the local community and beaches of San Diego. After the event, members grabbed a burger and a shake at our SIG Shake Shack Fundraiser.

Beach End of Year Bonfire

To celebrate the year of hard work and dedication, SIG held an End of the Year Beach Bonfire with pizza, drinks, snacks, and s'mores!
