Stock Pitch Competition

Join us for our annual Stock Pitch Competition where participants analyze companies through an ESG lens and present their investment thesis. This event brings together students from across campus to showcase their analytical skills, research capabilities, and presentation abilities.

Participants will have the opportunity to pitch sustainable investment opportunities, incorporating environmental, social, and governance factors into their analysis. The competition provides valuable experience in equity research, financial modeling, and public speaking while emphasizing the importance of responsible investing.

Winners will be recognized for their outstanding analysis and presentation skills, with opportunities for networking with industry professionals and SIG members.

Quantathon

Our Quantathon is a data science and quantitative analysis competition that challenges participants to solve real-world financial problems using programming, statistical modeling, and data analysis techniques. This event combines the excitement of a hackathon with the rigor of quantitative finance.

Teams will work on complex problems involving portfolio optimization, risk modeling, ESG data analysis, and algorithmic trading strategies. Participants will use Python, R, or other programming languages to develop solutions that integrate sustainability metrics into quantitative investment strategies.

The competition provides hands-on experience with financial data, programming tools, and quantitative methods while emphasizing the integration of ESG factors into quantitative models. It's an excellent opportunity for students interested in both finance and data science to showcase their skills and learn from peers.
