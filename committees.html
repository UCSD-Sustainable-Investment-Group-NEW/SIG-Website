Committee Overview

Each committee specializes in different aspects of sustainable investment analysis and portfolio management

Committee Photo

Equity Research Committee

Conducts in-depth analysis of publicly traded companies, evaluating financial data and industry trends with ESG-adjusted models to provide investment recommendations. Learn more →

Sustainability Committee

Focuses on qualitative evaluation of ESG factors—carbon emissions, waste management, DEI, and risk management—that influence long-term stock potential and market volatility. Learn more →

Quantitative Analysis Committee

Develops statistical models and programming solutions for portfolio construction, risk management, and trade execution. Integrates ESG factors into quantitative frameworks to drive data-driven investment decisions. Learn more →

Communications & Marketing

Plans SIG's internal logistics and external events, manages social media, creates promotional materials, and builds community through networking opportunities and marketing campaigns. Learn more →

Committee Photo

Research & Development (R&D)

Drives long-term innovation by turning ideas into projects, studying financial theory and ESG research, and developing SIG's ESG-efficient frontier investment philosophy through interdisciplinary research and technical implementation. Learn more →
